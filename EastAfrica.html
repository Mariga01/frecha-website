Tanzania

Tanzania is packed with some of the worlds greatest wonders,from Mount Kilimanjaro-the highest peak in Africa-to the vast endless plains of the Serengeti-Tanzanias oldest and most popular park, descend the depths of Ngorongoro Crater.

Uganda

Also known as the "Pearl of Africa"Uganda has it all.The Mountains of the Moon in Rwenzori National Park are the highest range in Africa.Distinct attractions include the tree climbing lions of Ishasa,Queen Elizabeth national parks and the wild water rafting of the River Nile.

Rwanda

Rwanda is a symbol of modern day miracle.Until 1996, Rwanda was locked in a brutal civil war and related conflicts. Since then, this beautiful country has been open to tourists. As a tourist you get to witness the mass slaughter of innocents. Macabre memorial sites scattered throughout the country mark the horrific genocide in 1994.
